Mind-e offers four plans to fit different needs. All paid plans include monthly credits, social media integrations, and priority support.

<Note>
  Yearly billing saves approximately 2 months compared to monthly billing.
</Note>

New accounts start on the **Free plan** with a one-time **100 credit** signup bonus.

## Upgrading

1. Go to your [dashboard settings](https://app.mind-e.co/dashboard/settings) or the [pricing page](https://mind-e.co/pricing)
2. Select a plan and billing interval (monthly or yearly)
3. Complete checkout through Paddle
4. Your new plan takes effect immediately

When you upgrade:
- Your agent limit increases immediately
- Any locked agents are automatically unlocked
- You receive the full monthly credit allowance for the new plan
- Storage limit increases immediately

## Downgrading

You can downgrade to a lower plan at any time. Downgrades can take effect at the end of your current billing period.

When you downgrade:
- **Excess agents are locked** — if you have more agents than the new plan allows, the least recently used agents are locked. Locked agents can't be edited or start new conversations, but their data is preserved.
- **Storage over limit** — if your storage usage exceeds the new plan's limit, you won't be able to upload new content until you're under the limit.
- **Integrations** — if you downgrade to the Free plan, all social media integrations (Instagram, WhatsApp, Telegram, Messenger) are disconnected.

<Warning>
  Before downgrading, check which agents will be locked and whether your storage is within limits. You can see this information in the downgrade confirmation screen.
</Warning>

### Undo a scheduled downgrade

If you scheduled a downgrade but change your mind, you can cancel it before it takes effect from your settings page.

## Canceling

1. Go to your [dashboard settings](https://app.mind-e.co/dashboard/settings)
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period, then reverts to Free

### Reactivating

If you've scheduled a cancellation, you can reactivate your subscription before the billing period ends. Your plan continues as if nothing changed.

## Invoices

View and download invoices for past payments through the Paddle billing portal, accessible from your settings page.
