---

Access your account settings from the [dashboard settings page](https://app.mind-e.co/dashboard/settings).

## Profile

Update your personal information:

| Field | Requirements |
|-------|-------------|
| First name | 2–50 characters |
| Last name | 2–50 characters |
| Avatar | JPEG, PNG, or WebP — max 5 MB |

Click **Save** to apply changes.

### Avatar

Upload a profile picture that appears in your dashboard. Supported formats are JPEG, PNG, and WebP with a maximum size of 5 MB. Uploading a new avatar automatically replaces the old one.

## Password

To change your password, enter your new password. Password requirements:

- 4–20 characters
- At least 1 uppercase letter
- At least 1 lowercase letter
- At least 1 digit
- At least 1 symbol (`!@#$%^&*`)

## Subscription

Your current plan and billing details are shown in the settings page. From here you can:

- View your current plan and billing cycle
- **Upgrade** to a higher plan
- **Downgrade** to a lower plan
- **Cancel** your subscription
- **Reactivate** a cancelled subscription
- Access the **billing portal** to manage payment methods and view invoices

See [Plans & Pricing](/account/plans-and-pricing) for more details.

## Authentication

Mind-e supports two authentication methods:

- **Email and password** — create an account with your email address
- **Google OAuth** — sign in with your Google account

If you signed up with Google, you can use the password reset flow to set a password for email-based login.
